# How To Use The Robot:
This section of team 19528's repository tells its members how to drive and control our robot

# Driving
Use left and right sticks on gamepad 1 to drive, up down left and right.

# Launcher
Press the right bumper to set speed to 30% (Button RB on gamepad 1)
Hold the right trigger to change speed from 30% to 60% (Trigger RT on gamepad 1)
THE RIGHT BUMPER DOES NOT NEED TO BE HELD DOWN TO USE THE RIGHT TRIGGER
